Unixx South Pattaya is a prominent high-rise condominium that has established itself as one of the most popular rental options in the southern end of Pattaya. Its location near the intersection of South Pattaya Road and Thepprasit Road gives residents easy access to both the nightlife of Walking Street and the quieter, more upscale atmosphere of Pratumnak Hill.

## Location

The building sits in a transitional zone between the energy of south-central Pattaya and the relative calm of Pratumnak. Walking Street is roughly a ten-minute walk or a five-minute Grab ride. Pratumnak Hill, with its restaurants, viewpoints, and quieter beaches, is accessible in the other direction within a similar timeframe.

Big C Extra, one of Pattaya's largest supermarkets, is practically next door, which is a genuine convenience for long-stay residents who want to shop for groceries without a long commute. Thepprasit Night Market, one of the best local markets for food and clothing, operates nearby on weekends.

## The Building

Unixx is a 45-story tower with a striking modern facade that makes it one of the more recognizable buildings on the Pattaya skyline. The higher floors offer panoramic views that sweep from Pattaya Bay around to Jomtien Beach, and on clear days you can see all the way to the islands offshore.

Units range from studios of approximately 24 square meters to two-bedroom apartments of around 70 square meters. The interiors feature a contemporary design with neutral tones, built-in furniture, and functional layouts. Most rental units come fully furnished with air conditioning, a TV, kitchenette, and WiFi.

## Facilities

The rooftop infinity pool is the standout facility. Positioned on one of the upper floors, it offers views that compete with some of Pattaya's best hotel pools. The pool area includes loungers, a children's section, and a covered seating area. A well-equipped fitness center provides modern cardio and weight equipment.

Additional amenities include a sauna, garden area, keycard security system, 24-hour CCTV, and a parking garage. The building management maintains common areas to a good standard, and the lobby is staffed around the clock.

## Rental Market

Unixx has one of the most active rental markets in Pattaya. Units are available on every major booking platform including Agoda, Booking.com, and Airbnb. Short-term nightly rates typically range from 900 to 2,000 THB depending on the unit size, floor level, and view orientation. Sea-view units command a premium but are worth it for the panoramic outlook.

Long-term monthly rentals are available from approximately 9,000 THB for a basic studio up to 25,000 THB for a furnished two-bedroom with a sea view. These rates are competitive for the quality of the building and its location.

## Living Experience

The resident mix at Unixx skews toward younger expats, digital nomads, and tourists on extended stays. The building has a social atmosphere without being noisy, and the common areas are well-used but not overcrowded. The rooftop pool is the natural gathering point, particularly during late afternoon when the sunset views are at their best.

The South Pattaya location means you are close to nightlife but not in the middle of it. This suits people who want to go out easily but return to a quieter environment at the end of the night.

## Who It Is Best For

Unixx South Pattaya is a strong choice for visitors who want a modern high-rise condo experience with excellent views and a convenient location. It works well for solo travelers, couples, and small groups, and the range of unit sizes means there is something for most budgets. The proximity to both Walking Street and Pratumnak Hill gives you flexibility in how you spend your time, and the active rental market means units are readily available for both short and long stays.
