Once Pattaya Wongamat is one of the most anticipated residential developments to arrive in Pattaya's luxury condo market. Situated directly on Wongamat Beach, widely regarded as the cleanest and most desirable stretch of sand in the greater Pattaya area, this new-build project targets buyers and renters who want premium finishes, modern facilities, and genuine beachfront living without the chaos of central Pattaya.

## The Development

The project comprises a single high-rise tower with a contemporary architectural design that emphasizes glass, clean lines, and open-plan living. Construction standards are notably above the typical Pattaya condo, with developers sourcing European-grade fixtures, imported stone countertops, and premium sanitary ware throughout. The lobby and common areas are finished to a standard that rivals five-star hotels, setting the tone from the moment you walk in.

Once Pattaya is a freehold development, which matters for foreign buyers looking at long-term investment. Units are available under both Thai and foreign quota, and the developer offers professional rental management services for owners who want to generate income from short-term or long-term lets.

## Unit Types

The development offers a range of configurations to suit different needs. Studios start at approximately 28 square meters, designed efficiently for solo travelers and digital nomads who want a compact but well-appointed base. One-bedroom units range from 35 to 50 square meters, offering a separate living area and full kitchen. The two-bedroom units, starting at around 65 square meters, are suitable for families or groups who need more space.

All units come with air conditioning, high-speed internet connectivity, and balconies that provide either sea views or garden views depending on floor and orientation. The higher floors deliver sweeping panoramas across the Gulf of Thailand that are genuinely spectacular at sunset.

## Facilities

The rooftop infinity pool is the headline feature. Positioned on the uppermost floor, it offers unobstructed views of the ocean and the Pattaya skyline. The pool deck includes sun loungers, a jacuzzi section, and a shaded relaxation area. A fully equipped fitness center sits adjacent to the pool level, featuring modern cardio and weight equipment.

Ground-level facilities include 24-hour security with CCTV coverage, key-card access throughout the building, dedicated visitor parking, and a reception area staffed during business hours. There is also a co-working lounge with high-speed WiFi, which has become increasingly important for the digital nomad demographic that Pattaya continues to attract.

## Location Advantage

Wongamat Beach is a five-minute walk from the building entrance. Unlike Pattaya Beach or Jomtien Beach, Wongamat remains relatively uncrowded and the water quality is noticeably better. Several beachfront restaurants and bars line the shore, offering fresh seafood and sunset drinks at reasonable prices.

The area is well-connected to the rest of Pattaya. Terminal 21 shopping mall is a ten-minute drive south. Central Marina is even closer. The Sanctuary of Truth, one of Pattaya's most impressive cultural landmarks, is within walking distance. For nightlife, central Pattaya and Walking Street are accessible by baht bus or Grab in 15 to 20 minutes.

## Tomorrowland 2026

For visitors planning around events, the Tomorrowland Thailand 2026 festival site is located in the greater Pattaya area. Wongamat's northern position means you avoid the worst of the traffic congestion that southern areas experience during major events, while still being within reasonable travel distance to festival grounds.

## Investment Potential

Pattaya's condo market has shown consistent rental yield growth over the past several years, driven by increasing tourist numbers and a growing expat community. Wongamat Beach commands premium rental rates due to its beach quality and quieter atmosphere. Studios and one-bedrooms in well-managed buildings in this area typically achieve occupancy rates above 70 percent during high season.

Once Pattaya's developer offers a guaranteed rental return program for the first two years, which provides income certainty for new buyers while the building establishes its reputation on booking platforms.

## How to Inquire

Units can be purchased directly from the developer's sales office on-site or through authorized real estate agents in Pattaya. For short-term rentals, units are listed on Agoda, Booking.com, and Airbnb through the building's management company. Long-term rentals of one month or more can be arranged directly through the management office, often at significantly reduced monthly rates compared to nightly bookings.

Prospective buyers should arrange a site visit to see the showroom units and the actual views from different floors. The sales team offers virtual tours for overseas buyers who cannot visit in person.
