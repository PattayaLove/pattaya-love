The Pullman Pattaya Hotel G is a five-star beachfront resort set in expansive tropical gardens on [Wongamat Beach](/areas/wongamat/) — one of the cleanest and quietest stretches of sand in the Pattaya area. Part of the Accor group, the Pullman delivers international chain consistency with a resort atmosphere that feels distinctly different from the high-rise hotels in central Pattaya.

## Location

The hotel sits on a private section of Wongamat Beach in North Pattaya, accessed via Naklua Road Soi 16. The setting is genuinely secluded — lush gardens, mature trees, and direct beach frontage create a resort bubble that could make you forget you are in Pattaya.

Terminal 21 shopping mall is about a 10-minute drive south. Central Pattaya's Beach Road and nightlife zones are reachable in 15 to 20 minutes by Grab or baht bus. The trade-off for the peaceful location is that you are not within walking distance of much beyond the hotel and beach, but most guests see that as the point.

## Rooms

The hotel has 340 rooms, all with private balconies — a feature that many Pattaya hotels at this price point lack. Rooms are bright with large windows and natural light, and come equipped with free Wi-Fi, minibar, safe, and free bottled water. Bathrooms include separate bathtub and rain shower.

The garden and pool-view rooms are the standard option. Sea-view rooms on higher floors command a premium but deliver on the promise — unobstructed views across Wongamat Beach and the Gulf of Thailand.

## Facilities

The resort layout spreads across manicured gardens rather than stacking everything into a tower, which gives the property a relaxed, horizontal feel.

- **Two outdoor swimming pools** — large freeform pools surrounded by sun loungers and tropical landscaping
- **Private beach access** — direct path from the hotel grounds onto Wongamat Beach
- **Spa** — full-service with Thai massage, aromatherapy, and wellness treatments
- **Fitness center** — modern equipment with garden views
- **Restaurants and bars** — multiple dining options on-site including international and Thai cuisine
- **Yoga classes** — scheduled sessions in the garden setting

## Who Is It For?

The Pullman appeals to couples and families who want a genuine resort experience rather than a city hotel. The beachfront location, spacious grounds, and two pools make it particularly good for longer stays where you want to spend time within the property. It is also a solid choice for visitors who want to explore Pattaya's nightlife and attractions by day but retreat to something quieter at night.

## Rates

Rooms start from around 3,500 to 6,000 THB per night depending on season and room type. For a five-star international chain with direct beach access and resort facilities, the pricing is competitive — comparable properties on Wongamat Beach are limited.
