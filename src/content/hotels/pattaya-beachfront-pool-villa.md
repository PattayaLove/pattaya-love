The Pattaya Beachfront Pool Villa represents the pinnacle of private accommodation in the Pattaya area. Located in Na Jomtien, south of the main tourist strip, this four-bedroom villa sits directly on the beach with a private pool, dedicated service staff, and the kind of space and exclusivity that defines genuine luxury. This is not a hotel room with a pool view — this is your own private beach house.

## The Property

The villa occupies a generous plot directly on the Na Jomtien beachfront, with the sand and sea literally steps from the pool deck. The architecture blends contemporary Thai design with tropical resort aesthetics: high ceilings, open-air living spaces, natural materials, and floor-to-ceiling glass that frames the ocean view from virtually every room.

Four bedrooms accommodate up to eight guests comfortably. The master suite is on the upper level with a private balcony overlooking the ocean, a king-size bed, walk-in closet, and a luxury en-suite bathroom with a freestanding bathtub. The three additional bedrooms each have en-suite facilities, air conditioning, and quality furnishings. All bedrooms feature premium mattresses and hotel-quality linens.

The living areas are expansive. An open-plan living room flows into a dining area that seats eight, and then out to the pool deck through sliding glass doors. The kitchen is fully equipped with professional-grade appliances, including a full-size refrigerator, oven, induction cooktop, dishwasher, and everything needed to prepare elaborate meals.

## Service Staff

What elevates this property from a simple rental to a true luxury experience is the included service staff. A dedicated housekeeper handles daily cleaning, laundry, and general upkeep. A cook can be arranged for an additional fee, preparing Thai and international meals using fresh ingredients from the local market. The property manager is available by phone for any requests or assistance during your stay.

This level of service means you can enjoy the privacy and independence of a villa while still benefiting from the convenience and pampering of a luxury resort.

## The Pool and Beach

The private infinity pool overlooks the beach, creating a seamless visual connection between the pool, the sand, and the ocean beyond. The pool deck includes sun loungers, a daybed, an outdoor shower, and a shaded dining area for al fresco meals. The beach is accessible through a gate in the garden, providing direct and private access to the sand.

Na Jomtien's beach is quieter and less developed than Pattaya Beach or even Jomtien Beach, which means fewer vendors, less noise, and a more genuinely relaxed coastal atmosphere. The water quality is also notably better this far south.

## Tomorrowland 2026

Na Jomtien's location is particularly advantageous for visitors planning to attend Tomorrowland Thailand 2026. The festival grounds are situated in the wider Pattaya-Jomtien area, and Na Jomtien provides a convenient base that avoids the congestion of central Pattaya during the event. Returning to a private beachfront villa after a day at one of the world's biggest music festivals is an experience that adds a memorable dimension to the trip.

## Pricing and Booking

A property of this caliber commands premium rates. Expect nightly pricing from approximately 15,000 to 25,000 THB depending on the season, with peak-season and holiday rates at the upper end. Weekly bookings typically attract a discount of 10 to 15 percent, and monthly rates can reduce the effective nightly cost further.

When divided among four couples or a group of six to eight friends, the per-person cost becomes surprisingly accessible for the level of luxury provided. The villa can be booked through select platforms including Airbnb Luxe and specialist villa rental agencies. Direct booking through the property management company often yields the best rates and the most flexibility.
