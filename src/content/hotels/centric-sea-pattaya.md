Centric Sea Pattaya occupies one of the most desirable positions in the city, situated on the beachfront side of Central Pattaya within walking distance of Central Festival mall. Developed by a well-known Thai developer, the twin-tower project delivers a premium condo experience that sits comfortably between the best mid-range offerings and full luxury developments.

## Location

The building is located on Pattaya Beach Road, which means the beach is literally across the street. Central Festival, Pattaya's largest shopping mall, is a five to seven minute walk south. The Hilton Pattaya, for reference, sits atop Central Festival, so Centric Sea shares essentially the same neighborhood advantages.

Beach Road provides easy access to baht buses that run the length of Pattaya for 10 THB, connecting you to Walking Street in the south and Naklua in the north. Grab rides to anywhere in the central area rarely exceed 100 THB. The location is genuinely central, and this is reflected in both rental demand and pricing.

## The Building

Centric Sea comprises two towers rising above a shared podium level that houses the main facilities. The architectural design is modern and visually striking, with glass facades that catch the light and give the building a premium appearance on the Pattaya skyline. Common areas are finished to a high standard with quality materials, contemporary artwork, and professional landscaping.

Units range from studios of approximately 25 square meters to two-bedroom apartments exceeding 80 square meters. The interior design across most units features a clean, contemporary aesthetic with neutral color palettes, quality flooring, and well-planned layouts. Higher-floor units with direct sea views are the most sought after, and justifiably so — the views across Pattaya Bay are outstanding.

## Facilities

The facility package at Centric Sea is comprehensive. Two rooftop swimming pools offer different atmospheres: one designed for active swimming with lanes, the other configured as a relaxation pool with ocean views and surrounding loungers. The rooftop level also includes a sundeck and social area.

The fitness center is above average for a Pattaya condo, featuring modern equipment from reputable brands. A sauna and steam room complement the gym for post-workout recovery. On the podium level, a landscaped garden area provides green space and a quieter alternative to the rooftop.

Security is robust, with 24-hour guards, keycard access at all entry points, CCTV throughout common areas, and a staffed reception desk. Parking is available in a dedicated garage beneath the building.

## Rental Options

Centric Sea has an active rental market across all durations. Short-term nightly rates on Agoda and Booking.com typically range from 1,500 to 3,500 THB depending on unit size, floor, and view. Sea-view studios on higher floors are the most popular booking category.

Long-term rentals of one month or more offer better value, with studios from approximately 12,000 to 20,000 THB per month and one-bedrooms from 18,000 to 30,000 THB per month. Premium two-bedroom units with full sea views can reach 40,000 to 50,000 THB per month.

These rates position Centric Sea in the luxury segment of the Pattaya condo market, but the beachfront location and quality of facilities justify the premium over inland alternatives.

## Living Experience

The resident community at Centric Sea includes a mix of well-established expats, visiting professionals, and tourists who prefer condo living over hotels. The atmosphere is more refined than budget-oriented condos, and the building management enforces noise and conduct policies that maintain a pleasant living environment.

The beachfront location means you wake up to sea views and can be on the sand within two minutes of leaving your door. Combined with the proximity to Central Festival's restaurants, shops, and cinema, daily life at Centric Sea is defined by convenience.

## Who It Is Best For

Centric Sea Pattaya is the right choice for visitors who want premium condo accommodation in the best possible central location. It suits couples, professionals, and anyone who prioritizes quality finishes, excellent facilities, and proximity to the beach. If you are comparing it to a luxury hotel at a similar price point, the condo offers more space, a kitchen, and greater independence, which makes it particularly appealing for stays of a week or longer.
