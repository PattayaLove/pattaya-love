## Overview

Utopia is a Soi 6 beer bar that promises a good time, and mostly delivers. It offers both a social bar area and upstairs facilities for those looking for more privacy.

## What to Expect

Utopia is a typical Soi 6 beer bar with room facilities above the bar. Private rooms and shower facilities are available. Staff are welcoming and the atmosphere is relaxed. It is a solid middle-of-the-road option on Soi 6 — nothing exceptional, but a reliable spot for a drink and some company.

## Prices

- **Beer:** 80 – 120 THB
- **Cocktails:** 150 – 250 THB
- **Lady drinks:** 150 – 200 THB
