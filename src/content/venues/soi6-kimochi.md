## Overview

Kimochi is a Japanese-themed beer bar on Soi 6, easily spotted by the large "Pattaya Soi 6" lettering on its sign. The pink and purple decor gives it a playful, eye-catching look that stands out from the surrounding bars. The name means "feeling" in Japanese.

## What to Expect

The interior leans into the Japanese aesthetic with colorful lighting and themed decor. Staff are friendly and the bar attracts a diverse crowd of international visitors. It sits next to VIP Bar, and the two make for an easy back-to-back visit. Drinks are standard Soi 6 prices.

## Prices

- **Beer:** 80 – 120 THB
- **Cocktails:** 150 – 250 THB
- **Lady drinks:** 150 – 200 THB
