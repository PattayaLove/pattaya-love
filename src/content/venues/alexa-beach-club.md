Alexa Beach Club sits directly on Jomtien Beach and has quickly established itself as Pattaya's flagship beach club destination. Combining a large swimming pool, beachfront lounging areas, a full-service bar, and a regular lineup of international and local DJs, Alexa brings a level of daytime-to-sunset entertainment that Pattaya has historically lacked.

## The Experience

The setup is what you would expect from a proper beach club: a freeform infinity pool facing the ocean, rows of padded sun loungers with dedicated table service, cabanas for groups who want a more private setup, and a swim-up bar where you can order without leaving the water. The design leans modern and clean, with white furniture, natural wood accents, and enough greenery to soften the concrete edges.

Music is the heartbeat of the operation. Resident DJs spin deep house and tropical house through the afternoon, building gradually from mellow poolside beats to fuller sets as sunset approaches. On weekends and holidays, Alexa brings in guest DJs from Bangkok and abroad, and the energy shifts noticeably. Saturday sunset sessions are the signature event, drawing a well-dressed crowd that treats the evening like a proper night out.

## Drinks and Food

This is a premium venue and the prices reflect it. Cocktails range from 290 to 450 THB, with signature creations featuring Thai ingredients like lemongrass, pandan, and fresh coconut. Beers start at 180 THB for local brands and 250 THB for imports. Bottle service is available for groups, with packages starting around 5,000 THB.

The food menu covers upscale bar food — think wagyu sliders, tuna tartare, Thai-inspired tapas, and wood-fired pizzas. Portions are designed for sharing, and most dishes run 250 to 550 THB. The kitchen maintains solid quality, and eating here eliminates the need to leave for dinner.

## When to Go

Alexa operates daily from around 11:00 to 23:00, though hours may extend on event nights. The sweet spot is arriving between 15:00 and 16:00. This gives you time to settle into a lounger, swim, work through a couple of cocktails, and be perfectly positioned for sunset, which typically hits between 18:00 and 18:30.

Weekdays are more relaxed and easier to secure good loungers without a reservation. Weekends, especially Saturdays, are busier and reserving a daybed or cabana in advance is recommended if you are coming with a group.

## The Crowd

Alexa attracts a mix of well-off Thai visitors from Bangkok, expats celebrating occasions, tourists looking for something beyond the typical Pattaya bar scene, and couples seeking a more upscale daytime experience. The dress code is smart casual — swimwear is fine at the pool, but cover up if you head to the restaurant area.

The atmosphere is upscale without being exclusive. You will not feel out of place in board shorts and a decent shirt. What Alexa offers is a polished alternative to the beer-and-bar-stool routine that dominates much of Pattaya's social scene.

## Getting There

Alexa Beach Club is located along Jomtien Beach Road, roughly 15 minutes south of central Pattaya by car. A Grab ride from Walking Street costs 100 to 150 THB. The venue has its own parking for those driving. If you are staying in Jomtien, it is likely within walking or short motorbike distance. Look for the prominent signage on the beachside of Jomtien Beach Road.
