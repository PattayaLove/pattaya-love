## Overview

Foxy Bar is a friendly and approachable beer bar on Soi 6 with a name that sets the tone. It is a reliable spot for a casual drink and easy company throughout the afternoon and evening.

## What to Expect

Foxy Bar is a typical Soi 6 open-air beer bar. Private rooms and shower facilities are available upstairs. The atmosphere is warm and welcoming, with staff who are genuinely friendly and good at making visitors feel at ease. It draws a mix of first-timers and returning visitors.

## Prices

- **Beer:** 80 – 120 THB
- **Cocktails:** 150 – 250 THB
- **Lady drinks:** 150 THB
