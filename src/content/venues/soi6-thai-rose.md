## Overview

Thai Rose is a modestly named beer bar on Soi 6 with a gentler vibe than many of its neighbours. The name suggests something softer, and the bar lives up to that with a more laid-back approach.

## What to Expect

Thai Rose is a typical Soi 6 open-air beer bar. Shower facilities are available, though there are no private rooms. The atmosphere is calm and welcoming, with staff who are pleasant and low-key. It appeals to visitors who prefer a slightly quieter spot on the soi.

## Prices

- **Beer:** 80 – 120 THB
- **Cocktails:** 150 – 250 THB
- **Lady drinks:** 150 THB
