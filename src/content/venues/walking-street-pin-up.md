Pin Up A Go Go is widely regarded as one of the best GoGo bars on Walking Street, and it has held that reputation for years. Located at 547/18 Walking Street, in the middle stretch where the biggest venues cluster, Pin Up consistently draws large crowds and delivers a level of entertainment that justifies the hype.

## The Atmosphere

Pin Up's interior is polished and well-maintained. The venue is spacious with a large central stage, elevated VIP sections, and comfortable seating throughout. The design leans into a classic pin-up aesthetic with retro-inspired decor, but the overall feel is modern and upscale by Walking Street standards.

The lighting and sound system are a cut above most competitors. The music mix tends toward high-energy Western tracks, and the volume is loud enough to feel immersive without making it impossible to talk to the person next to you.

The crowd is a mix of tourists, expats, and returning visitors. Pin Up attracts people who have been to Walking Street before and know that this is one of the venues worth prioritizing.

## The Dancers

This is where Pin Up truly separates itself. The dancers are consistently rated among the best on Walking Street, and many seasoned Pattaya visitors place them at a solid 9 out of 10 compared to the competition.

The performers are well-rehearsed, energetic, and clearly enjoy what they do. Shows rotate throughout the night and range from group choreography to individual spotlight performances. The quality is maintained across the evening, though the best shows tend to come between 22:30 and midnight when the house is fullest.

Pin Up is known for attracting dancers who genuinely invest in their stage presence, and it shows. If entertainment quality is your top priority when choosing a GoGo bar, Pin Up is the answer.

## VIP Lounge

Pin Up offers a VIP area on the upper level with a more exclusive feel. VIP seating comes with a drink package and a better vantage point over the main stage. Pricing for VIP varies but typically starts from around 1,500 to 2,500 THB per person including drinks.

The VIP section is worth considering if you are celebrating a special occasion or simply want a more comfortable, less crowded experience. You can also book VIP in advance through the venue's social media pages.

## Drink Prices

Pin Up's pricing is in line with other top-tier GoGo bars on Walking Street.

- **Bottled beer (Singha, Chang, Leo):** 170 to 200 THB
- **Draught beer:** 100 to 140 THB
- **Cocktails:** 220 to 350 THB
- **Lady drinks:** 180 to 200 THB
- **Spirits (bottle):** 2,000 to 4,000 THB

There is no cover charge. You pay for what you drink.

## What to Expect

Walk in, and a hostess will seat you. Service is prompt, and staff are friendly without being overly aggressive. If a dancer comes to sit with you between sets, buying a lady drink is the customary gesture, but nobody will force the issue.

The bar fine at Pin Up runs between 1,000 and 2,000 THB depending on the time of night.

Be prepared for the venue to get very crowded after 23:00 on weekends. If you arrive early (around 20:30 to 21:00), you will have your pick of seats and can watch the venue gradually fill to capacity.

## Tips for Visitors

- **Arrive early for the best seats.** The spots closest to the stage go first.
- **Carry cash.** While some premium venues on Walking Street accept cards, cash is faster and avoids any surcharge.
- **Tipping dancers on stage** is common and appreciated. A 20 to 100 THB note placed on the stage or handed directly is standard.
- **Check the bill carefully** before paying. Pin Up's billing is generally accurate, but it is a good habit everywhere on Walking Street.

## The Verdict

Pin Up A Go Go has earned its status as a Walking Street institution. The combination of exceptional dancers, a well-designed venue, and a consistently electric atmosphere makes it one of the top two or three GoGo bars in all of Pattaya. If you only visit one GoGo bar during your trip, Pin Up should be at the top of the list.
