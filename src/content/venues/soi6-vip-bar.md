## Overview

VIP Bar is a straightforward beer bar on Soi 6, positioned right next to the Japanese-themed Kimochi. Despite the name, this is a no-frills drinking spot with standard prices and a welcoming atmosphere. It keeps things simple and does it well.

## What to Expect

Open-front layout with bar seating and a few tables. Staff are attentive and the crowd is mostly international visitors exploring the soi. The location next to Kimochi makes it easy to find. A good choice for a quick beer or a longer evening session without any pretension.

## Prices

- **Beer:** 80 – 120 THB
- **Cocktails:** 150 – 250 THB
- **Lady drinks:** 150 – 200 THB
