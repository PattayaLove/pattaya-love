Baccara is one of Walking Street's legacy venues. It has been operating for decades and consistently ranks among the top GoGo bars in Pattaya across forums, review sites, and word of mouth. That kind of longevity on a street where bars open and close like revolving doors says something about the quality of the operation.

## What to Expect

The venue is well-sized with a central stage and comfortable seating arranged so that most positions give you a decent view of the entertainment. The interior is dark and moody in the way you would expect from a GoGo bar, but it is clean, well-maintained, and clearly managed by people who care about the product.

Shows are the main draw. Baccara invests in its entertainment, and it shows. Choreographed stage performances rotate throughout the evening, and the dancers are enthusiastic and professional. The production quality is a step above many competitors, and regular visitors note that the lineup changes often enough to keep things fresh.

The atmosphere is lively without being chaotic. Staff are welcoming and efficient, and there is a sense that the venue runs on a system rather than improvisation. First-timers will find Baccara approachable, and experienced visitors already know why they keep coming back.

## Drink Prices

Beer runs around 160 to 180 THB for a bottle. Cocktails sit in the 200 to 300 THB range. Lady drinks are approximately 180 to 200 THB. Pricing is competitive for Walking Street and there are no hidden charges to worry about as long as you pay attention to your bill.

There is no cover charge. You are free to walk in, grab a seat, and decide at your own pace.

## Tips

Arrive between 20:30 and 21:30 for the best seat selection before the peak crowds. The shows hit their best between 22:00 and midnight, so being settled in early means you get the full experience without scrambling for a spot.

Baccara is an excellent choice if you are visiting a GoGo bar for the first time and want a reliable experience. The quality is consistent, the staff are not pushy, and the overall vibe is welcoming.

Keep smaller bills handy for drinks and tips. Tipping dancers who catch your eye is customary and appreciated, with 20 to 100 THB being the normal range.

Even long-established venues like Baccara can be affected by seasonal closures or renovations. Confirming it is open before making it the centerpiece of your evening plan is always sensible, particularly during the low season months.
