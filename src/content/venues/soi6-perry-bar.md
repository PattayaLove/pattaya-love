## Overview

Perry Bar is a casual beer bar on Soi 6 that keeps a low profile compared to some of the bolder-named establishments nearby. It offers a straightforward drinking experience without the noise.

## What to Expect

Perry Bar is a typical Soi 6 open-air beer bar. No private rooms — this is a straightforward drinking spot. The atmosphere is calm and friendly, with staff who are welcoming and easy to talk to. A good option for those who prefer a quieter drink on the soi.

## Prices

- **Beer:** 80 – 120 THB
- **Cocktails:** 150 – 250 THB
- **Lady drinks:** 150 THB
