## Overview

Byblos Beach Bar & Restaurant is one of Walking Street's most unique venues — a beachfront bar and restaurant that sits directly on the sand at the southern end of Pattaya's famous party strip. While most Walking Street venues are indoor, neon-lit affairs, Byblos offers something genuinely different: cocktails with your feet in the sand, ocean breeze, and a sunset view that makes you forget you're in the middle of Thailand's party capital.

**Address:** 216-2 Pattayasainueang, Walking Street, Pattaya City, Bang Lamung District, Chon Buri 20150

## What to Expect

Byblos operates as a multi-purpose venue that transitions through the day:

**Afternoon (16:00 – 18:00):** Relaxed beach bar atmosphere. Grab a sunbed, order a cocktail, and watch the sunset over the Gulf of Thailand. This is the golden window — Walking Street is still quiet, the light is perfect, and the vibe is pure Mediterranean beach club.

**Evening (18:00 – 22:00):** The restaurant comes alive with a full food menu. International and Thai cuisine served on the beachfront terrace. Live music starts, typically acoustic sets or chill bands that complement the setting without overwhelming conversation.

**Night (22:00 – 02:00):** The energy shifts up as DJs take over. The beach bar transforms into a proper party venue while Walking Street behind you hits full throttle. It's the best of both worlds — beach party energy with club-level music, but open-air and relaxed.

## Live Music & DJs

Music is central to the Byblos experience. The venue hosts regular live bands and rotating DJs playing everything from tropical house and deep house to funk and Latin beats. The sound system is serious without being overbearing — designed for a beach setting, not a concrete box.

Special themed nights and events are announced on their social media. During high season (November to February), expect bigger acts and busier nights.

## Accommodation

One of Byblos' unique selling points — they offer rooms directly at the venue. Falling asleep to the sound of waves after a night on Walking Street, then waking up to a beach sunrise, is an experience few other venues in Pattaya can offer.

Rooms are limited, so booking in advance is recommended, especially during peak season and holidays.

## Private Events & Weddings

Byblos has become a popular venue for private events, corporate parties, and destination weddings. The beachfront setting with Walking Street as the backdrop creates a genuinely memorable event location.

- **Weddings:** Beachfront ceremony and reception packages available
- **Private parties:** Full venue buyout possible for larger events
- **Corporate events:** Customizable packages with catering and AV equipment

Contact the venue directly for event inquiries and pricing.

## Drink Prices

Expect beach club pricing — higher than a standard Walking Street beer bar, but reasonable for the setting:

- **Cocktails:** 280 – 450 THB
- **Beer:** 180 – 220 THB
- **Wine:** From 250 THB/glass
- **Shisha:** Available

## Tips

- **Arrive for sunset** (around 17:30 – 18:00) for the best experience. The transition from golden hour to nightlife is magical
- **Reserve a sunbed** on weekends and holidays — the beachfront fills up fast
- **Dress code:** Relaxed. Beach attire is perfectly fine during the day, smart casual works for evening
- **Combine it:** Start at Byblos for sunset, then walk straight into Walking Street for the rest of the night — the location couldn't be more convenient
- **Tomorrowland 2026:** Expect Byblos to host pre-parties and special events during Tomorrowland Thailand in November 2026

## Who It's For

Couples looking for a romantic Walking Street alternative. Groups wanting a more sophisticated starting point for their night out. Event planners seeking a unique beachfront venue. Anyone who thinks "beach bar on Walking Street" sounds too good to be true — it's real, and it's excellent.
