## Overview

LIQUID is one of the more polished-looking bars on Soi 6, with a clean setup and a name that suggests you should be drinking. It attracts visitors who appreciate a slightly more put-together beer bar experience.

## What to Expect

LIQUID is a typical Soi 6 open-air beer bar. Private rooms and shower facilities are available upstairs. The atmosphere is upbeat and sociable, with staff who are attentive and know how to work the bar. It has a somewhat more modern feel compared to some of the older establishments on the soi.

## Prices

- **Beer:** 80 – 120 THB
- **Cocktails:** 150 – 250 THB
- **Lady drinks:** 150 THB
