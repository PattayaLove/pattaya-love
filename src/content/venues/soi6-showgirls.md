## Overview

Showgirls is a beer bar on Soi 6 that puts its staff front and centre. The name promises entertainment, and the bar delivers with an outgoing team and an atmosphere that is more performative than some of its quieter neighbours.

## What to Expect

Showgirls is a typical Soi 6 open-air beer bar. Private rooms and shower facilities are available upstairs. The atmosphere is entertaining and upbeat, with staff who are confident and know how to work the crowd. It stays open until 02:00, and the energy builds as the night goes on.

- **Beer:** 80 – 120 THB
- **Cocktails:** 150 – 250 THB
- **Lady drinks:** 150 THB
