## Overview

Where Angels Play is one of the more poetically named bars on Soi 6 and has earned a solid reputation among regulars. It is a well-run establishment that consistently delivers a good experience.

## What to Expect

Where Angels Play is a typical Soi 6 open-air beer bar. Private rooms and shower facilities are available upstairs. The atmosphere is friendly and welcoming, with staff who are attentive and genuinely engaging. It is often recommended by repeat visitors as one of the better bars on the soi.

## Prices

- **Beer:** 80 – 120 THB
- **Cocktails:** 150 – 250 THB
- **Lady drinks:** 150 THB
