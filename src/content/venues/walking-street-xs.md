XS A Go Go is one of the newer-generation GoGo bars on Walking Street, and it brings a distinctly modern energy to a strip where many venues rely on the same formula. The vibe here leans more toward a nightclub atmosphere, with sharp lighting, a quality sound system, and a crowd that tends to skew younger than some of the more established bars nearby.

## The Atmosphere

XS feels fresh. The interior is clean and contemporary, with good use of LED lighting and a layout that puts the stage front and center without cramming everyone into tight rows. It is not the biggest venue on Walking Street, but the compact size works in its favor, creating an intimate and energetic feel once the place fills up.

The music leans toward modern club tracks and EDM, which fits the overall brand. If you are looking for something that feels less like a traditional GoGo bar and more like a high-energy lounge, XS delivers that hybrid experience.

## Entertainment and Dancers

The shows at XS are well-produced and regularly updated. Dancers bring genuine enthusiasm, and the younger roster gives the performances a dynamic, high-energy quality. The choreography is tight, and the overall production has clearly been thought through.

Peak performance times start later than most venues, around 22:30 to 23:00, reflecting the club-oriented identity. The bar opens at 21:00, and the first hour tends to be a warm-up with fewer performers.

## Drink Prices

- **Bottled beer:** 160 to 190 THB
- **Draught beer:** 90 to 130 THB
- **Cocktails:** 200 to 300 THB
- **Lady drinks:** 170 to 200 THB

No cover charge. Pricing is competitive and consistent with mid-tier Walking Street venues.

## Tips for Visitors

XS is a strong option if you have already seen the big-name GoGo bars like Pin Up and Iron Club and want something with a different feel. Arrive after 22:00 for the best experience, and grab a seat near the stage before the late-night rush.

The staff are welcoming without being pushy, and the overall management keeps things running smoothly. It is a reliable choice for a good night out with a modern twist on the classic Walking Street experience.
