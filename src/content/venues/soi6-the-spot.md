## Overview

The Spot is a well-known beer bar on Soi 6 that lives up to its name — it's a popular gathering point for regulars and newcomers alike.

## What to Expect

A typical Soi 6 open-air beer bar with a welcoming atmosphere and staff who make you feel at home. The vibe is relaxed and social, making it an easy place to start your afternoon on the soi.
