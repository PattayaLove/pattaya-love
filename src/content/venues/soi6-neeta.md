## Overview

Neeta on Soi 6 is a beer bar recognized by its eye-catching neon-style sign. Positioned next to Baba Bar and Bandits Hideout, it sits in a lively stretch of the soi where foot traffic flows steadily throughout the evening. A solid local favorite with a welcoming atmosphere.

## What to Expect

The neon signage draws attention and the interior keeps things comfortable with typical Soi 6 bar seating. Staff are sociable and regulars mix with first-time visitors. The location between Baba Bar and Bandits Hideout makes it part of a natural three-bar crawl for those exploring the area.

## Prices

- **Beer:** 80 – 120 THB
- **Cocktails:** 150 – 250 THB
- **Lady drinks:** 150 – 200 THB
