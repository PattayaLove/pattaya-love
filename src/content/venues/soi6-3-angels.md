## Overview

3 Angels Bar is a well-known beer bar on Soi 6 that stays open later than most of its neighbours. The bar draws a steady crowd of regulars and first-timers who appreciate its straightforward atmosphere and friendly staff.

## What to Expect

3 Angels Bar is a typical Soi 6 open-air beer bar. Private rooms and shower facilities are available upstairs. The atmosphere is lively, with staff who are welcoming and keep the energy going well into the early hours. With closing at 02:00, it is one of the later options on the soi.

## Prices

- **Beer:** 80 – 120 THB
- **Cocktails:** 150 – 250 THB
- **Lady drinks:** 150 THB
