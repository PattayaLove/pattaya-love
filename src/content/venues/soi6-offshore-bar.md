## Overview

Offshore Bar is a well-established beer bar on Soi 6 with a name that resonates with the many expats and offshore workers who visit Pattaya during their time off. It has a loyal customer base and a consistent atmosphere.

## What to Expect

Offshore Bar is a typical Soi 6 open-air beer bar. Private rooms and shower facilities are available upstairs. The atmosphere is social and welcoming, with staff who are experienced and know how to look after regulars. Open until 02:00, it is a reliable late-night option.

- **Cocktails:** 150 – 250 THB
- **Lady drinks:** 150 THB
