## Overview

Tiffany's Bar has one of the longest operating windows on Soi 6, opening at 10:00 and staying open until 02:00. That 16-hour stretch means it catches everyone from morning drinkers to late-night visitors.

## What to Expect

Tiffany's Bar is a typical Soi 6 open-air beer bar. No private rooms — this is a straightforward drinking spot. The atmosphere shifts throughout the day, starting quiet in the morning and building energy as the evening crowd arrives. Staff are consistent and keep the bar running smoothly across the long hours.

## Prices

- **Beer:** 80 – 120 THB
- **Cocktails:** 150 – 250 THB
- **Lady drinks:** 150 THB
