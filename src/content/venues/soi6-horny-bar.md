## Overview

Horny Bar wears its name proudly and is one of the more recognisable spots on Soi 6. It has a straightforward approach — cold drinks, friendly staff, and no pretences about what kind of bar it is.

## What to Expect

Horny Bar is a typical Soi 6 open-air beer bar. Private rooms and shower facilities are available upstairs. The atmosphere is lively and direct, with staff who are upfront and sociable. The bar's bold branding tends to catch the eye of visitors walking the soi for the first time.

## Prices

- **Beer:** 80 – 120 THB
- **Cocktails:** 150 – 250 THB
- **Lady drinks:** 150 THB
