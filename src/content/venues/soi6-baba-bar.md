## Overview

Baba Bar is the flagship of the Baba Group on Soi 6. It positions itself a step above the average beer bar with more carefully selected staff and a slightly more polished environment.

## What to Expect

Part of the Baba Group — known for higher-quality staff and slightly premium pricing (lady drinks from 200 THB). Baba Bar attracts punters willing to pay a small premium for a better overall experience. The girls are well-presented and the service is attentive. The bar itself is clean and well-maintained, with a noticeably more upscale feel than many Soi 6 neighbours.

## Prices

- **Beer:** 80 – 120 THB
- **Cocktails:** 150 – 250 THB
- **Lady drinks:** 200 THB
