## Overview

Fire Bar is a well-managed Soi 6 beer bar that has built a solid reputation for having some of the best rooms on the street. It draws visitors who appreciate quality facilities alongside a good bar atmosphere.

## What to Expect

Fire Bar is known for its excellent rooms with en-suite showers — widely regarded as some of the best on Soi 6. Private rooms and shower facilities are available. There is no minimum lady drink requirement, which sets it apart from some competitors. The bar is well-run with friendly, professional staff and a clean environment throughout.
