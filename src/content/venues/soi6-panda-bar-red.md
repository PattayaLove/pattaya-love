## Overview

Panda Bar Red is part of the Panda Bar chain on Soi 6, easily identified by its red and pink theme and Korean text signage. Located near Ruby Club, it is one of two Panda bars on the soi, each with its own color identity. This one opens early at 14:00 for afternoon drinkers.

## What to Expect

The red and pink decor creates a warm, lively atmosphere. Korean influence shows in the signage and occasional music choices. Staff are welcoming and the bar draws a mix of Korean and international visitors. Open until 02:00, it covers both the afternoon and late-night crowd.

## Prices

- **Beer:** 80 – 120 THB
- **Cocktails:** 150 – 250 THB
- **Lady drinks:** 150 – 200 THB
