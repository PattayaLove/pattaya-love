## Overview

Kawaii Bar brings a touch of cute Japanese-inspired flair to Soi 6. The name means "cute" in Japanese, and the bar leans into that theme with its presentation and the cheerful attitude of its staff.

## What to Expect

Kawaii Bar is a typical Soi 6 open-air beer bar. Private rooms and shower facilities are available upstairs. The atmosphere is light and playful, with staff who are among the more cheerful on the soi. It stands out slightly from its neighbours thanks to its themed branding.

## Prices

- **Beer:** 80 – 120 THB
- **Cocktails:** 150 – 250 THB
- **Lady drinks:** 150 THB
