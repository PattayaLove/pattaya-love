## Overview

Roxy Bar is a well-known fixture on Soi 6 with a strong social media presence and a reputation for a good time. It is one of the more active bars on the soi, regularly posting updates and drawing in visitors.

## What to Expect

Roxy Bar is a typical Soi 6 open-air beer bar. Private rooms and shower facilities are available upstairs. The atmosphere is vibrant and sociable, with staff who are photogenic and active on the bar's social channels. It stays open until 02:00, making it a solid late-night option.

## Prices

- **Beer:** 80 – 120 THB
- **Cocktails:** 150 – 250 THB
- **Lady drinks:** 150 THB
