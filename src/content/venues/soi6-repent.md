## Overview

Repent is a beer bar on Soi 6 with an ironic name that fits the street perfectly. Opening at 16:00 and running until 02:00, it caters to the evening and late-night crowd rather than the afternoon drinkers.

## What to Expect

Repent is a typical Soi 6 open-air beer bar. Private rooms and shower facilities are available upstairs. The atmosphere is spirited and fun, with staff who lean into the bar's cheeky branding. It picks up energy as the evening progresses and the soi gets busier.

## Prices

- **Beer:** 80 – 120 THB
- **Cocktails:** 150 – 250 THB
- **Lady drinks:** 150 THB
