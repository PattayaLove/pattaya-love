## Overview

Soho Bar borrows its name from London's famous entertainment district and brings a simple, unpretentious drinking experience to Soi 6. It is a familiar fixture on the soi for anyone who has visited more than once.

## What to Expect

Soho Bar is a typical Soi 6 open-air beer bar. No private rooms — this is a straightforward drinking spot. The atmosphere is relaxed and casual, with staff who are friendly and approachable. A reliable choice for a quiet drink without too much commotion.

## Prices

- **Beer:** 80 – 120 THB
- **Cocktails:** 150 – 250 THB
- **Lady drinks:** 150 THB
