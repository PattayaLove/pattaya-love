## Overview

BTS Bar stands out on Soi 6 with its K-pop theme, blue frontage, and a large BTS billboard that makes it impossible to miss. Positioned between the "Resist Everything But Temptation" bar and "in Love" bar, it caters to fans of Korean pop culture and curious walk-ins alike.

## What to Expect

The Korean pop influence runs through the decor and music selection. Staff are friendly and the atmosphere is upbeat and fun. The blue-themed interior gives it a different energy from the typical Soi 6 beer bar. A good conversation starter and a unique stop on the soi.

## Prices

- **Beer:** 80 – 120 THB
- **Cocktails:** 150 – 250 THB
- **Lady drinks:** 150 – 200 THB
