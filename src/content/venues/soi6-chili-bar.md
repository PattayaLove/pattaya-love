## Overview

Chili Bar opens earlier than most Soi 6 venues and stays open until 02:00, giving it one of the longest operating windows on the soi. The bar has a spicy reputation and a loyal following among regulars.

## What to Expect

Chili Bar is a typical Soi 6 open-air beer bar. Private rooms and shower facilities are available upstairs. The atmosphere is lively and energetic, with staff who know how to keep the party going. Opening at 14:00 makes it a solid option for those who start their afternoon early.

## Prices

- **Beer:** 80 – 120 THB
- **Cocktails:** 150 – 250 THB
- **Lady drinks:** 150 THB
