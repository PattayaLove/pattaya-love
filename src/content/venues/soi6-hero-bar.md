## Overview

Hero Bar is a straightforward beer bar on Soi 6 that does not try to be anything other than what it is — a simple spot for a drink and some company. It blends into the soi's lineup without drawing too much attention.

## What to Expect

Hero Bar is a typical Soi 6 open-air beer bar. No private rooms — this is a straightforward drinking spot. The atmosphere is friendly and unassuming, with staff who are pleasant and keep things moving without pressure. A quiet option relative to some of the louder bars on the soi.

## Prices

- **Beer:** 80 – 120 THB
- **Cocktails:** 150 – 250 THB
- **Lady drinks:** 150 THB
