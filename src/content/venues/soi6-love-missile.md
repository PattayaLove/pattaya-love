## Overview

Love Missile has one of the more memorable names on Soi 6 and lives up to it with an energetic atmosphere. It is a bar that does not hold back on personality or attitude.

## What to Expect

Love Missile is a typical Soi 6 open-air beer bar. No private rooms — this is a straightforward drinking spot. The atmosphere is lively and sociable, with staff who are outgoing and keep the banter flowing. The bar's bold name draws curious first-timers walking the soi.

## Prices

- **Beer:** 80 – 120 THB
- **Cocktails:** 150 – 250 THB
- **Lady drinks:** 150 THB
