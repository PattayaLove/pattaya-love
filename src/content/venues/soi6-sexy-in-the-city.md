## Overview

Sexy in the City plays on the famous TV show name and applies it to the Soi 6 context. It is one of the more recognisable bar names on the soi and draws visitors in with its bold signage and confident staff.

## What to Expect

Sexy in the City is a typical Soi 6 open-air beer bar. Private rooms and shower facilities are available upstairs. The atmosphere is confident and lively, with staff who are sociable and keep the conversation going. It stays open until 02:00 and tends to be busier during peak evening hours.

## Prices

- **Beer:** 80 – 120 THB
- **Cocktails:** 150 – 250 THB
- **Lady drinks:** 150 THB
