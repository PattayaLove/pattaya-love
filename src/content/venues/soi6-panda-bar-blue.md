## Overview

Panda Bar Blue is the sister bar to Panda Bar Red, forming the second half of the Panda chain on Soi 6. The bar features a distinctive checkered black and white lower facade with Korean and Thai text signage. It sits near Booties and Miyu, making it part of a lively cluster.

## What to Expect

The blue-themed interior contrasts with its red sibling and gives the bar its own personality. Like Panda Red, it opens early at 14:00 and runs until 02:00. Staff are friendly and the Korean-Thai vibe attracts an international mix. A good option for afternoon beers or late-night drinks.

## Prices

- **Beer:** 80 – 120 THB
- **Cocktails:** 150 – 250 THB
- **Lady drinks:** 150 – 200 THB
