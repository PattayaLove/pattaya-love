## Overview

Miyu is a Japanese and Korean themed beer bar on Soi 6, distinguished by its sign featuring both Korean and Chinese characters. The name and decor lean into an East Asian aesthetic that gives it a unique identity among the soi's mostly Western-oriented bars.

## What to Expect

The themed interior creates a distinct atmosphere with Asian-influenced decor and music. Staff are attentive and the bar draws visitors curious about its different look and feel. Located near Panda Bar Blue and Booties, it is part of a cluster with a noticeable East Asian influence.

## Prices

- **Beer:** 80 – 120 THB
- **Cocktails:** 150 – 250 THB
- **Lady drinks:** 150 – 200 THB
