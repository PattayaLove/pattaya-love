## Overview

Desire Bar is a well-presented beer bar on Soi 6 that opens at 14:00, catching the early afternoon crowd. It has built a solid reputation for attentive service and a welcoming vibe.

## What to Expect

Desire Bar is a typical Soi 6 open-air beer bar. Private rooms and shower facilities are available upstairs. The atmosphere is friendly and inviting, with staff who are attentive without being pushy. A good choice for a relaxed afternoon session that can stretch into the evening.

## Prices

- **Beer:** 80 – 120 THB
- **Cocktails:** 150 – 250 THB
- **Lady drinks:** 150 THB
