Republic fills a niche on Walking Street that not many other venues cover. While most clubs on the strip lean toward EDM and commercial dance music, Republic is the go-to spot for hip hop, R&B, and urban beats. If you have been bouncing between clubs and craving something with more groove and less bass drop, this is where you want to be.

## What to Expect

The interior is relatively compact compared to the mega clubs further down the street, but that works in Republic's favor. The smaller footprint creates a more intimate, high-energy atmosphere where the dance floor feels alive even when the venue is not completely full.

DJs focus on hip hop classics, current R&B, and urban mixes, with the occasional dancehall or afrobeats set thrown in. The music selection is one of the best curated on Walking Street if EDM is not your thing.

The crowd leans younger and more international. You will find a good mix of tourists from various countries, Thai locals who are into the hip hop scene, and expats who prefer this sound over the standard club formula. The vibe is generally relaxed and fun, with people actually dancing rather than just standing around with drinks.

## Drink Prices

Beer prices hover around 150 to 180 THB. Cocktails range from 200 to 300 THB, with some signature mixes going a bit higher. There is usually no cover charge, which makes it easy to pop in and check the vibe before committing to a full night.

Look out for drink specials earlier in the evening. Republic sometimes runs promotions to get people through the door before the peak hours hit.

## Tips

The best time to arrive is around 23:00 to midnight. This gives you a chance to settle in before the dance floor really fills up. By 01:00, Republic typically hits its stride and the atmosphere is at its best.

Republic is a good option if you are traveling with a mixed group that has different music tastes. The hip hop focus makes it stand out, and it pairs well with a club-hopping itinerary that includes one of the EDM-heavy venues earlier or later in the night.

As with all Walking Street venues, it pays to verify that Republic is currently operating before building your evening around it. Venues on the strip have been known to rebrand, renovate, or take breaks, especially during the quieter months of the year.
