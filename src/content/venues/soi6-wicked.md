## Overview

Wicked opens at 16:00 and leans into its name with an edgier vibe than some of the more cheerful bars on Soi 6. It attracts visitors who appreciate a bar with a bit of attitude and personality.

## What to Expect

Wicked is a typical Soi 6 open-air beer bar. Private rooms and shower facilities are available upstairs. The atmosphere is bold and spirited, with staff who match the bar's edgy branding. It stays open until 02:00, and the later hours tend to be the liveliest.

## Prices

- **Beer:** 80 – 120 THB
- **Cocktails:** 150 – 250 THB
- **Lady drinks:** 150 THB
