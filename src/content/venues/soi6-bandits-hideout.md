## Overview

Bandits Hideout is a beer bar on Soi 6 sporting bold orange and red signage that stands out along the strip. Located right next to Neeta, it offers a fun, slightly cheeky vibe that matches its outlaw-themed name. The bar has carved out a loyal following among Soi 6 regulars.

## What to Expect

The bar leans into its playful branding with themed decor and a relaxed, social atmosphere. Staff are friendly and the open-front design makes it easy to grab a seat and watch the soi go by. Pairs naturally with neighboring Neeta for a two-stop session.

## Prices

- **Beer:** 80 – 120 THB
- **Cocktails:** 150 – 250 THB
- **Lady drinks:** 150 – 200 THB
