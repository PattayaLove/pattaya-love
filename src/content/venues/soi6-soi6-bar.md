## Overview

Soi 6 Bar takes its name directly from the street it calls home. Part of the Baba Group, it offers a straightforward but slightly elevated beer bar experience with quality staff and clean surroundings.

## What to Expect

Part of the Baba Group — known for higher-quality staff and slightly premium pricing (lady drinks from 200 THB). Soi 6 Bar is a solid representation of what the Baba Group aims for — attractive, well-mannered staff in a tidy venue. The atmosphere is social and welcoming, with a touch more polish than the average Soi 6 spot.
