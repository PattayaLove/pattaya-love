Myst positions itself as the upscale option on Walking Street, and the higher price point comes with a noticeably more polished experience. Located near the far end of the strip, the club aims for a Bangkok-level nightlife atmosphere transplanted into Pattaya, and on a good night it delivers exactly that.

## What to Expect

The interior is a clear step up from the standard Walking Street club. Better lighting design, cleaner decor, and a sound system that hits hard without distorting at high volumes. The layout includes VIP sections with bottle service, a main dance floor with good sightlines to the DJ booth, and enough bar space that ordering a drink does not feel like a contact sport.

Music ranges from EDM and progressive house to commercial remixes, with DJs who generally know how to read the room. On weekends and holidays, guest DJs from Bangkok and internationally occasionally take the booth, which elevates the experience further.

The crowd at Myst tends to skew slightly older and more affluent than the average Walking Street club. You will see well-dressed groups, people celebrating occasions, and visitors who specifically chose Myst for the upgrade. That said, the door policy is relaxed by international standards. Smart casual is fine, and no one is getting turned away for wearing shorts in Pattaya.

## Drink Prices

This is where the premium positioning shows. Beer starts around 180 to 200 THB, cocktails run from 300 to 400 THB, and bottle service packages are available for groups willing to spend more. Prices are higher than the neighboring clubs, but the quality of drinks and service generally justifies the difference.

VIP table packages can be good value if you are in a group of four or more, as they often include a bottle and mixers.

## Tips

Arrive around 23:00 if you want to secure a good position without the peak-hour crush. The club reaches full swing between midnight and 02:00. If VIP seating is important to you, consider booking in advance on busy weekends or during holiday periods.

Myst is one of the better venues on Walking Street for a special occasion or a night when you want something a bit more refined than the usual options. It pairs well with dinner at one of the nearby restaurants before heading in.

Walking Street venues evolve constantly, and even established clubs can change management or concept. Checking Myst's current status and any event listings before heading out is worthwhile, especially if you are planning to spend at the higher end.
