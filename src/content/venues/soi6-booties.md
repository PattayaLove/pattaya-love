## Overview

Booties is a beer bar on Soi 6 recognized by its bold red signage. Situated above the Panda Bar Blue area, it occupies a visible spot on the soi and benefits from the foot traffic drawn by the surrounding cluster of bars including Miyu and the Panda chain.

## What to Expect

A straightforward Soi 6 beer bar with a fun name and no-nonsense approach. Staff are sociable and the open-front setup invites walk-ins. The bar keeps standard hours and pricing, making it an easy stop while exploring the soi. Busiest in the early evening hours.

- **Beer:** 80 – 120 THB
- **Cocktails:** 150 – 250 THB
- **Lady drinks:** 150 – 200 THB
