## Overview

Officially "Bee Corner Bar", this is one of Soi 6's most recognizable landmarks — a large open-air bar on a prime corner position with a distinctive red banner. It's also one of the earliest opening bars on the soi, often serving customers before most other bars have even opened their shutters.

## What to Expect

Corner Bar is a proper sports bar with free pool tables, free Wi-Fi, TVs showing live sport, and a food menu alongside the drinks. No private rooms — this is a straightforward drinking and socializing spot. The atmosphere is friendly and relaxed, with great music and staff who know every regular by name. Its early opening makes it a gathering point for the Soi 6 morning and afternoon crowd.

## Prices

- **Beer:** 80 – 120 THB
- **Cocktails:** 150 – 250 THB
- **Lady drinks:** 150 THB
