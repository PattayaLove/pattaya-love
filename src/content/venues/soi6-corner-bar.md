## Overview

Corner Bar sits at a prime corner location on Soi 6 and opens at 10:00, making it one of the earliest bars on the soi. It is a familiar landmark for anyone who has spent time in the area.

## What to Expect

Corner Bar is a typical Soi 6 open-air beer bar. No private rooms — this is a straightforward drinking spot. The atmosphere is friendly and relaxed, with staff who know the regulars well. Its early opening makes it popular with daytime drinkers and those looking for a cold beer before the rest of the soi wakes up.

## Prices

- **Beer:** 80 – 120 THB
- **Cocktails:** 150 – 250 THB
- **Lady drinks:** 150 THB
