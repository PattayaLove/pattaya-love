## Overview

Candy Bar is a no-frills beer bar on Soi 6 offering cold drinks and easy conversation. It keeps things simple and unpretentious, which is exactly what many visitors on the soi are looking for.

## What to Expect

Candy Bar is a typical Soi 6 open-air beer bar. No private rooms — this is a straightforward drinking spot. The atmosphere is relaxed and casual, with staff who are happy to chat and keep your glass full. A good option if you want a quiet drink without the upsell.

## Prices

- **Beer:** 80 – 120 THB
- **Cocktails:** 150 – 250 THB
- **Lady drinks:** 150 THB
