## Overview

Oppa Bar brings a Korean-inspired twist to Soi 6 as part of the Baba Group. The name borrows from Korean culture, and the bar caters to a mixed crowd including Korean and international visitors.

## What to Expect

Part of the Baba Group — known for higher-quality staff and slightly premium pricing (lady drinks from 200 THB). Oppa Bar has a distinct Korean-style atmosphere that sets it apart from the typical Soi 6 beer bar. Staff are attractive and well-presented. The bar draws a diverse clientele and offers a slightly more refined experience than the standard open-front bars.
