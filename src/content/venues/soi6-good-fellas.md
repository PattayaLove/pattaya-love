## Overview

Good Fellas is a laid-back beer bar on Soi 6 with a name that nods to the regulars who keep coming back. It offers a no-nonsense drinking experience without any gimmicks.

## What to Expect

Good Fellas is a typical Soi 6 open-air beer bar. No private rooms — this is a straightforward drinking spot. The atmosphere is relaxed and social, with staff who are easy-going and make conversation flow naturally. A solid choice for those who prefer a simple, honest beer bar.
