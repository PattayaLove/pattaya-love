## Overview

Butterfly Bar is a colourful and inviting spot on Soi 6 that lives up to its name with a bright, cheerful setup. It is a reliable choice for an afternoon or evening drink in a relaxed setting.

## What to Expect

Butterfly Bar is a typical Soi 6 open-air beer bar. Private rooms and shower facilities are available upstairs. The atmosphere is friendly and laid-back, with staff who make newcomers feel comfortable. It tends to get busier in the evening hours as the soi fills up.

## Prices

- **Beer:** 80 – 120 THB
- **Cocktails:** 150 – 250 THB
- **Lady drinks:** 150 THB
