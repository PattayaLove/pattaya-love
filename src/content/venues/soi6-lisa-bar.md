## Overview

Lisa Bar is a simple, unpretentious beer bar on Soi 6 that keeps things straightforward. Named after its owner or a key staff member, it has the personal touch that some of the larger bars lack.

## What to Expect

Lisa Bar is a typical Soi 6 open-air beer bar. No private rooms — this is a straightforward drinking spot. The atmosphere is warm and personal, with staff who treat regulars like old friends. It is a quieter option for those who want a drink without too much noise or pressure.

## Prices

- **Beer:** 80 – 120 THB
- **Cocktails:** 150 – 250 THB
- **Lady drinks:** 150 THB
