## Overview

O Bar keeps things minimal, right down to its name. It is a no-frills beer bar on Soi 6 where the focus is on cheap drinks and casual conversation rather than flashy theming.

## What to Expect

O Bar is a typical Soi 6 open-air beer bar. No private rooms — this is a straightforward drinking spot. The atmosphere is relaxed and unpressured, with staff who are friendly without being overbearing. A good choice for visitors who want a quiet beer without the hard sell.

## Prices

- **Beer:** 80 – 120 THB
- **Cocktails:** 150 – 250 THB
- **Lady drinks:** 150 THB
