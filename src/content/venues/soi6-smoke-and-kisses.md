## Overview

Smoke and Kisses has one of the more evocative names on Soi 6, and the bar matches it with a slightly moodier atmosphere than some of the brighter spots nearby. It opens at 16:00, targeting the evening crowd.

## What to Expect

Smoke and Kisses is a typical Soi 6 open-air beer bar. Private rooms and shower facilities are available upstairs. The atmosphere is intimate and laid-back, with staff who are relaxed and engaging. It hits its stride later in the evening and stays open until 02:00 for the night owls.

## Prices

- **Beer:** 80 – 120 THB
- **Cocktails:** 150 – 250 THB
- **Lady drinks:** 150 THB
