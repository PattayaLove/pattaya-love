## Overview

Exotica is one of the more recognisable names on Soi 6, with a bar front that catches the eye and a reputation that keeps people coming back. It stays open until 02:00, making it a solid late-night option.

## What to Expect

Exotica is a typical Soi 6 open-air beer bar. Private rooms and shower facilities are available upstairs. The atmosphere is vibrant and upbeat, with staff who are outgoing and keep the energy high. It tends to be busier than some of its neighbours, particularly during peak evening hours.

## Prices

- **Beer:** 80 – 120 THB
- **Cocktails:** 150 – 250 THB
- **Lady drinks:** 150 THB
