## Overview

Tomcat's is a beer bar on Soi 6 with a name that needs no explanation. It has a confident, unapologetic feel and draws visitors who know what they are looking for on the soi.

## What to Expect

Tomcat's is a typical Soi 6 open-air beer bar. Private rooms and shower facilities are available upstairs. The atmosphere is bold and social, with staff who are direct and engaging. It stays open until 02:00, making it a popular choice for the late-night crowd.
