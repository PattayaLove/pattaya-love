## Overview

Fahrenheit A Go Go is one of Walking Street's established GoGo bars, located in the first section of the strip near the Soi Diamond intersection. It has built a solid reputation for consistent entertainment and a lively atmosphere.

## What to Expect

The bar features multiple stages with rotating dancers and regular shows throughout the evening. The interior is modern with good lighting and sound. Staff are professional and the atmosphere is energetic without being aggressive.

## Drink Prices

- **Beer:** 160 – 180 THB
- **Cocktails:** 250 – 350 THB
- **Lady drinks:** 200 – 250 THB

## Tips

Fahrenheit is a good mid-range GoGo option — quality entertainment without the premium pricing of the top-tier venues. Arrive before 22:00 for a seat with a good view of the stage.
