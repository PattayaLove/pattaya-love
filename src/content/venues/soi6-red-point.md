## Overview

Red Point Bar is one of Soi 6's old-school closed-door establishments. It operates behind a shut front, giving it a more private and intimate feel compared to the typical open-front bars on the street.

## What to Expect

Red Point is a traditional closed-door Soi 6 bar with a more discreet atmosphere. Private rooms and shower facilities are available, and the rooms here are considered among the better options on the soi. The closed-door format means less street noise and more privacy. Staff are experienced and the bar has a loyal following among repeat visitors who appreciate the old-school approach.

## Prices

- **Beer:** 80 – 120 THB
- **Cocktails:** 150 – 250 THB
- **Lady drinks:** 150 – 200 THB
