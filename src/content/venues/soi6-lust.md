## Overview

Lust is a beer bar on Soi 6 that embraces its provocative name. It has a darker, moodier setup than some of its neighbours and attracts visitors who appreciate a bar with a bit of edge.

## What to Expect

Lust is a typical Soi 6 open-air beer bar. Private rooms and shower facilities are available upstairs. The atmosphere is seductive and laid-back, with staff who are confident and engaging. It offers a slightly different mood compared to the brighter, louder bars on the soi.

## Prices

- **Beer:** 80 – 120 THB
- **Cocktails:** 150 – 250 THB
- **Lady drinks:** 150 THB
