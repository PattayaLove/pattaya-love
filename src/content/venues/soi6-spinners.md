## Overview

Spinners is a recently renovated bar on Soi 6 with a distinctive black frontage. Located next to Horny Bar, it opened with colorful balloon decorations signaling a fresh start. The bar is still finding its feet but already draws curious visitors checking out the new setup.

## What to Expect

The renovated interior is clean and modern by Soi 6 standards. Staff are keen to build a regular crowd, so expect attentive service and a welcoming vibe. Spinners stays open until 02:00, making it one of the later-closing options on the soi for those who want to keep the night going.

## Prices

- **Beer:** 80 – 120 THB
- **Cocktails:** 150 – 250 THB
- **Lady drinks:** 150 – 200 THB
