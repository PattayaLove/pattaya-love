## Overview

Ruby Club opens at 13:00, making it one of the earliest bars on Soi 6. Despite the "Club" in the name, this is a beer bar through and through — the name just adds a touch of class to the Soi 6 experience.

## What to Expect

Ruby Club is a typical Soi 6 open-air beer bar. Private rooms and shower facilities are available upstairs. The atmosphere is relaxed during the early afternoon and picks up as evening approaches. Staff are friendly and accommodating. Its early opening makes it a go-to for daytime visitors.

## Prices

- **Beer:** 80 – 120 THB
- **Cocktails:** 150 – 250 THB
- **Lady drinks:** 150 THB
