## Overview

Ma-Mao Bar is a laid-back beer bar on Soi 6, conveniently located next to the Perry Kaffeine coffee shop. The bar keeps things simple with a friendly atmosphere, cold drinks, and an open-front setup typical of the soi. A solid pick for an easy afternoon session.

## What to Expect

Relaxed and unpretentious, Ma-Mao draws a mix of regulars and walk-ins. Staff are welcoming and the vibe stays casual throughout the evening. The location next to Perry Kaffeine makes it easy to spot and a natural stop for anyone working their way along the soi.

## Prices

- **Beer:** 80 – 120 THB
- **Cocktails:** 150 – 250 THB
- **Lady drinks:** 150 – 200 THB
