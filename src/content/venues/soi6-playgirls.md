## Overview

PlayGirls is a high-energy beer bar on Soi 6 that consistently ranks among the busier spots on the soi. The name sets expectations, and the bar delivers with an upbeat atmosphere and attentive staff.

## What to Expect

PlayGirls is a typical Soi 6 open-air beer bar. Private rooms and shower facilities are available upstairs. The atmosphere is lively and entertaining, with staff who are outgoing and keep the mood fun. Open until 02:00, it draws a late-night crowd that keeps the place buzzing.
