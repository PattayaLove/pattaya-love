Peppermint sits on the clubs end of Walking Street, closer to the beach and the mega nightclubs. Its location makes it a natural stop for visitors working their way down the full length of the strip, and it rewards those who make the trek past the busier middle section with a well-run GoGo bar experience.

## What to Expect

The venue is well laid out with a central stage, comfortable seating, and a design that feels more modern than some of the older establishments on the street. Peppermint clearly invests in keeping the place looking fresh, which contributes to an overall impression of quality.

Entertainment follows the familiar GoGo bar format — rotating dancers on stage, periodic show performances, and a soundtrack of Thai and Western music. The shows are solid without being over the top, and the dancers maintain a good energy level throughout the evening. The atmosphere is friendly and approachable, with staff who are professional and pleasant without being overbearing.

Being located near the clubs end of Walking Street has its advantages. The crowd at Peppermint often includes people who are warming up for the nightclubs nearby, which gives the venue a slightly more energetic and party-ready vibe, especially later in the evening.

## Drink Prices

Beer prices are around 150 to 180 THB for bottles. Cocktails sit in the 200 to 300 THB range. Lady drinks are approximately 150 to 200 THB. Standard Walking Street pricing with no surprises.

No cover charge applies, making it easy to walk in and check it out without any commitment.

## Tips

Peppermint works well as part of a Walking Street itinerary that starts with beer bars and GoGo bars in the early evening before transitioning to the nearby nightclubs after midnight. Its location makes this a natural progression.

If the middle-of-the-street GoGo bars are too packed on a busy night, walking the extra distance to Peppermint can pay off. The further end of Walking Street tends to be slightly less congested, and venues here sometimes have more seating available.

Arrive around 21:00 to 22:00 for the best combination of atmosphere and seat availability. The venue picks up steam as the evening progresses and peaks around 23:00 when the pre-club crowd is at its most energetic.

Like every Walking Street venue, Peppermint's operating status can shift with the seasons and business conditions. Confirming it is open before heading that way is always a good idea, especially if you are visiting during the quieter months when some venues reduce hours or close temporarily.
