## Overview

MOD'S Bar is a well-known name on Soi 6 with a strong following. It stays open until 02:00 and consistently draws a good crowd throughout the evening and into the late hours.

## What to Expect

MOD'S Bar is a typical Soi 6 open-air beer bar. Private rooms and shower facilities are available upstairs. The atmosphere is energetic and social, with staff who are experienced and know how to keep guests entertained. Its late closing time makes it a popular last stop for many visitors.

- **Cocktails:** 150 – 250 THB
- **Lady drinks:** 150 THB
