## Overview

Lollipop is a playfully named beer bar on Soi 6 that does not take itself too seriously. It has a fun, carefree vibe that matches the overall energy of the soi.

## What to Expect

Lollipop is a typical Soi 6 open-air beer bar. Private rooms and shower facilities are available upstairs. The atmosphere is fun and easygoing, with staff who are playful and keep the mood light. It is a good option for visitors looking for a relaxed time without any formality.

## Prices

- **Beer:** 80 – 120 THB
- **Cocktails:** 150 – 250 THB
- **Lady drinks:** 150 THB
