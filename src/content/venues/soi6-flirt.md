## Overview

Flirt opens a little later than most Soi 6 bars at 16:00, catering to the evening crowd rather than afternoon drinkers. It makes up for the later start by staying open until 02:00.

## What to Expect

Flirt is a typical Soi 6 open-air beer bar. Private rooms and shower facilities are available upstairs. The atmosphere is lively and playful, matching the bar's name. Staff are sociable and keep the mood light. A good pick if you are heading to the soi in the late afternoon or evening.

## Prices

- **Beer:** 80 – 120 THB
- **Cocktails:** 150 – 250 THB
- **Lady drinks:** 150 THB
